## Welcome to El Guacal

This is the first post on the El Guacal blog. We built this project to help the Venezuelan diaspora in the United Kingdom find the products they miss from home.

## What's next

We're working on improving the map experience and adding more stores. If you know a shop that sells Venezuelan products, you can add it directly through the app.

Stay tuned for more updates.
