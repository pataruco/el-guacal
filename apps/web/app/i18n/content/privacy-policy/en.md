## Visitors to the site

El Guacal collects anonymous information, such as which pages you visit, when you visit, which site sent you here, your browser type, and what content you requested. We use this information to offer better services to users.

We will not associate any data gathered from this site through navigation with any personally identifying information from any source.

## Contributors to the site

Contributors to the El Guacal website are asked to sign in using their email through Google Firebase Auth. This lets us track how many and where contributions come from, show how the community values the project, improve our user interfaces, protect our data from misuse, and identify users if they add inappropriate content, whether on purpose or by accident.

Only El Guacal maintainers can see a full list of a contributor’s activities to help manage the data.

Should you choose to send an email to <a href="mailto:hola@elguacal.com">hola@elguacal.com</a>  with your feedback, it will be stored again. We will keep it linked to your comments and might contact you to follow up. You are not required to reply or continue the conversation.

If you want to know what information we have about you, ask us to delete it, or request any changes, please contact us at <a href="mailto:hola@elguacal.com">hola@elguacal.com</a>. You can also use this address to exercise your rights under the GDPR.
