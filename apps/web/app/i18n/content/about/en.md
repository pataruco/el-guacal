---
title: About El Guacal
---

El Guacal is a community-driven store finder that helps you locate Venezuelan products across the United Kingdom. Whether you're looking for Harina P.A.N., Maltín Polar, or any other taste of home, El Guacal maps the shops that carry them.

## How it works

The data is collected and maintained by the community. Anyone can add a new store or update existing information to keep the map accurate and useful.

## Open data

All store and product data is available as a free download in CSV and JSON formats. Visit the [Dataset](/en/dataset) page to get the latest export.
